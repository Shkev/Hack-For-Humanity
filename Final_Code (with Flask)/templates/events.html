Veterans Housing and Homelessness Prevention Program Workshop

Info

Organization: California Department of Housing and Community Development

VHHP finances the development of affordable rental and transitional housing for veterans with an emphasis on housing for veterans experiencing homelessness and extremely low-income veterans. The program is administered by HCD in collaboration with the California Department of Veterans Affairs (CalVet) and Housing Financing Agency (CalHFA).

California Transition Assistance Program Mesa College

Info

Organization: California Transition Assistance Program (CalTAP)

WALK-INS WELCOME!
The California Department of Veterans Affairs---California Transition Assistance Program (CalTAP) offers a full array of transition assistance and serves to connect Veterans, Service Members, and their families with California specific resources geared to ensure a successful transition into civilian life. CalTAP would like to invite student veterans to attend our upcoming seminar which will highlight benefits and services available to veterans and their family members.

Dance for Vets

Info

Organization: SDSalsaBachata and Veterans who Salsa

SDSalsaBachata and Veterans who Salsa present "Dance for Vets", a charitable dance social. All proceeds of the ticket sales will go to support organiazations that provide services to homeless veterans.
Dance lessons begin at 8:30 pm by Vera from Vedanza Dance Company and Violeta Salsera.
